Kimchi—one of the oldest and probably the most essential dishes in korean cuisine. Kimchi is spicy and sour dish made up of fermented vegetable. It is prepared with various kinds of ingredients. But the common main dishes is cabbage. Kimchi is popular among foreigners for it's unique flavor, as well as it's hight nutritional value, fiber content and low calorie content. However, for koreans, it is most popular due to it's significant value. Without kimchi, dinner is considered incomplete.

Bibimbap—is essentially a bowl of mixed ingredients including, but not limited to rice, namul(seasoned and sauteéd vegetables) mushrooms, beef, soy sauce, gochujang(chili pepper paste), and a fried egg. The ingredients found in bibimbap vary by region, and the most famous versions of the dish are found in jeonju, tongyeong and jinju.





Bulgogi(marinated beef barbeque)—A juicy, savory dish of grilled marinated beef. bulgogi is one of the most popular korean meat dishes through out the world, and was rank as the 23rd most delicious food in the world according to CNN travel's reader's poll in 2011. It is often grilled with garlic and sliced onion to add flavor to the meat. The meat usually wrapped in lettuce and it is also traditionally eaten with ssamjang(thick, red spicy paste)

Japchae(stir-fried noodles)—often served as a side dish during lunch or dinner, japchae is a traditional korean noodle dish made up of stiry fried sweet potato, thinly shredded vegetable, beef, and a hint of soy sauce and sugar. Depending on the chef, additional ingredients like mushrooms are added to the mix. Japchae is known for it's sweet and flavorful taste and it's soft yet slightly chewy texture.





Ddukbokki(spicy rice cake)—ddukbokki is also spelled tteokbokki, is a common spicy korean food made of cylindrical rice cakes, triangular fish cake, vegetable, and sweet red chili sauce. It is often soldy by pojangmacha(street vendors) people enjoy ddeukbokki for the combination of sweet and spicy flavors

Samgyeopsal(pork strips)—one of the most popular korean dishes in south korea, samgyeopsal consists of grilled slices of pork belly meat that are not marinates or seasoned. They are commonly dipped in seasoning made of salt and pepper mixed in sesame seed oil, and then wrapped in lettuce along with grilled slices of gralic, grilled slices of onion, shredded green onions, and kimchi. It is one of the most common dishes found in any korean restaurant throughout the world.





Seolieongtang(ox bone soup)—A traditional hot korean soup maden from ox bones, ox meats nd brisket, seolieongtang is a local dish of seoul, often seasoned with salt, ground black pepper, copped greep onions or minced garlic according to the consumer taste. The broth is of a milky white, cloudy color and often eaten with rice. Sseolieongtang is known for it's soft and chewy texture and flavorful broth and can be found inmost koeean restaurant

Sundubu-jjigae or soft tofu stew is a jjigae in Korean cuisine. The dish is made with freshly curdled soft tofu, vegetables, sometimes mushrooms, onion, optional seafood, optional meat, and gochujang or gochu garu.
